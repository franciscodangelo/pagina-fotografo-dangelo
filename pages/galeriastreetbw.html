STREET B-W

A percentage of my work feeds on the symbols and materialities of the street, not only as a compulsive photographic record of what is presented to me, but also transcends conceptual and aesthetic inquiries that are represented in other works. The works are also born from questions about myself and personal concerns, attempts to answer or representations of the same questions. binding lines could be degradation, deterioration, appropriation, error, glitch, putrefaction, visualizing everything in a future time from now, time, disappearance.

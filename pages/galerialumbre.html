LUMBRE

The act of photographing as an act of survival and the result of these images as a representation of a search surrounded by anxieties, uncertainty, shadows and a splitting and breaking of realities whose borders were fading more and more. My outings, my walks, my meetings happened in street view almost as a perfect substitution for my usual photographic experience, which at times was overwhelmed by access to multiple places/people/moments instantly, maintaining uncertainty and surprise/satisfaction in the meeting.
